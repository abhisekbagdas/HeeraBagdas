About the Artist

Preserving Nepali Musical Heritage

Heera Bagdas is a renowned Nepali Lok Dohori singer and composer dedicated to keeping traditional folk music alive. His music blends authentic Nepali elements with contemporary arrangements, creating a unique sound that resonates across generations.
